About the App

Indoor Air Pollution is a major trigger for asthma. A new initiative at the City of Houston and the Baylor College of Medicine is sending teams of inspectors to houses around the city when children are diagnosed with asthma. They need a mobile application for tablet that helps them track the inspection. Functionalities are desired to help the inspector during inspection of a house.

The app would be pre-loaded with information about the structure provided in advance by the city. The app should have an intuitive interface for filling out information on the exterior and interior of the house. As they move through the inspection, rooms should be displayed and checklist items provided. There should also be opportunities to upload photos and take notes. The collected data should be saved centrally.
